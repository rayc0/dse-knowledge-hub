# About — DSE 大全

## Who maintains this hub

This Knowledge Hub is autonomously written and maintained by **[CompanyForge](https://companyforge.ai)** — an AI operator platform that runs multiple knowledge hubs and businesses concurrently. The editorial team is a combination of AI agents and human editors who fact-check against primary sources.

## Mission

To be **the most useful, free, plain-language reference for Hong Kong DSE and university admission** — particularly for mainland students applying to HK universities, who currently have to wade through outdated forum threads, paid 留学中介 websites, and 200-page official PDFs.

## Why free

We believe educational information should be public. But — running this hub costs server time, AI API costs, and human editor hours.

We monetize through **dedicated commercial services** that are clearly separated from editorial content:

1. **Free 1-on-1 咨询** — first 30-minute consultation for mainland students
2. **Paid PS / personal statement editing service** — by HK university students + admission counselors
3. **Paid 一条龙服务** — full application support (PS + interview prep + visa + accommodation)
4. **Affiliate revenue** — when readers proceed to use partner services (留学中介, language schools, accommodation), we may receive commission

**Editorial firewall:**
- Educational article body content is **brand-neutral** and cites primary sources only
- Commercial services appear ONLY in dedicated landing pages, footers, and the About page
- We will publish information about competitors' services if asked (PRs welcome)

## Data sources

We cite primary sources for every factual claim:

- **HKEDB** (Education Bureau) — DSE syllabus, exam structure, scoring
- **JUPAS** — central university admission scoring
- **Each university's admission office** — Non-JUPAS requirements, fees, scholarships
- **HKEAA** — past papers, exam reports
- **UGC** — funding allocations, university statistics
- **QS / THE / ARWU** — international university rankings
